# Everyday Life

In this topic I describe my **daily routine** – what I do on a normal school day and how my weekends are different. It sounds simple, but the examiner will ask follow-up questions, so it is good to have details ready.

---

## My working day (school day)

- I usually **get up** at about 6:30. I'm not a morning person, so I often use the **snooze button**.
- I have a quick **breakfast** (tea and a roll), brush my teeth, get dressed and leave home.
- I go to school **by tram / by bus / on foot**; the journey takes about half an hour.
- Lessons start at 8 a.m. We usually have six or seven lessons a day with a **lunch break**.
- I have lunch in the **school canteen** or I buy something on the way.
- In the afternoon I sometimes stay at school for **practical training** in the graphic studio.
- When I come home, I **relax** for a while, then I do my **homework** and prepare for tests.
- In the evening I have dinner with my family, watch series, play games or chat with friends online.
- I try to **go to bed** before midnight – but I don't always succeed.

## Weekends and free time

- At the weekend I **sleep in** (sometimes until noon).
- I meet my **friends**, we go to the cinema or to a café ([[culture-leisure/cultural-life]]).
- I help at home with **household chores**: I tidy my room, wash the dishes, take out the rubbish or walk the dog.
- I work on my **hobbies** – drawing and graphic design projects, sport, music.
- Some students have a **part-time job (brigáda)** at the weekend.
- On Sunday evening I prepare my things for school – and complain that the weekend was too short.

## Time management and habits

- School days are quite **stereotyped** – the same routine repeats.
- I try to find a **balance** between school, hobbies, friends and sleep ([[culture-leisure/healthy-lifestyle]]).
- My biggest enemy is **procrastination** – I sometimes leave homework to the last minute.
- Modern life is fast; phones and social media take a lot of our time.

## Questions to practise

- Describe your typical school day from morning to evening.
- How do you get to school and how long does it take?
- What do you usually do in the evening?
- How is your weekend different from a working day?
- Do you help with any household chores? Which ones do you hate?
