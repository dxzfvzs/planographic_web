# Healthy Lifestyle

Health is the most important thing we have – and a lot of it depends on **how we live**. A healthy lifestyle means good **food, enough exercise and sleep**, and avoiding **bad habits**.

---

## Healthy eating

- A **balanced diet**: plenty of **fruit and vegetables**, wholegrain food, proteins (meat, fish, eggs, beans), dairy; not too much sugar, salt and fat.
- **Drinking enough water** – instead of sweet fizzy drinks and energy drinks.
- Regular meals; breakfast is important – it starts the day ([[culture-leisure/food-meals]]).
- Problems of today: **fast food and junk food**, snacking, eating in a hurry, too much sugar → **obesity** is growing, especially among children.
- Some people choose special diets: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free.

## Exercise and sport

- Doctors recommend at least **30 minutes of movement a day**: walking, cycling, running, swimming, gym, team sports.
- Sport keeps us **fit**, helps against stress and improves mood (endorphins!).
- The problem of our generation: we **sit too much** – at school, at the computer, with the phone; graphic designers spend all day at a screen, so compensation is important.
- You don't have to be an athlete – taking the **stairs instead of the lift** or walking to school counts too.

## Sleep and mental health

- Teenagers need about **8 hours of sleep**; screens before bed make falling asleep harder (blue light).
- **Stress** – from school, exams, social media pressure; we should relax, spend time with friends and in nature, and talk about problems.
- **Mental health** is as important as physical health – it is OK to ask for help (school counsellor, psychologist).

## Bad habits – what to avoid

- **Smoking** (including e-cigarettes/vaping) – causes cancer and heart disease,
- **alcohol** and **drugs** – dangerous especially for young people,
- too much **screen time** and social media, gambling, energy drinks,
- prevention: regular **check-ups** at the doctor's and dentist's, vaccination.

## Useful vocabulary

- **balanced diet** – vyvážená strava
- **junk food** – nezdravé jídlo
- **obesity** – obezita
- **to keep fit / to work out** – udržovat se v kondici / cvičit
- **bad habit** – zlozvyk
- **to give up smoking** – přestat kouřit
- **check-up** – preventivní prohlídka
- **mental health** – duševní zdraví

## Questions to practise

- What does a healthy lifestyle mean to you?
- Do you eat healthily? What did you eat yesterday?
- Do you do any sports or exercise? How often?
- How much time do you spend in front of a screen? Do you think it's too much?
- What bad habits are dangerous for young people?
