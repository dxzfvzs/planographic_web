# Cultural Life

Culture is everything that entertains us and makes us think: **films, theatre, music, art and museums**. In this topic I talk about what cultural events I attend, and about cultural life in my town.

---

## Cinema and films

- Going to the **cinema** is the most popular cultural activity for young people – multiplexes in shopping centres, popcorn, big screen; in summer **open-air cinemas**.
- Film genres: comedy, action, thriller, horror, sci-fi, fantasy, animated films, documentaries, romantic films.
- Today most people watch films at home on **streaming services** (Netflix, HBO, Disney+) – cheaper and comfortable, but the cinema is a social **experience**.
- Czech film festival: **Karlovy Vary International Film Festival**.

## Theatre, music and concerts

- **Theatre** offers plays (drama, comedy), **musicals, opera and ballet**; people usually dress up; Brno has several theatres (the National Theatre Brno, Městské divadlo).
- I can buy tickets online or at the **box office**; cheaper student tickets are often available.
- **Concerts and music festivals** – from classical music (Brno Philharmonic) to rock and pop festivals (Colours of Ostrava, Rock for People; in the UK Glastonbury).
- Music genres: pop, rock, hip hop, electronic music, jazz, classical music.

## Museums, galleries and art

- **Museums** show history, science and technology; **galleries** show paintings and sculptures.
- In London many museums are **free** ([[countries-cities/london]]); in Czechia there are discounts for students and free days; **Museum Night** is popular.
- As a graphic design student I am interested in **visual art, design and photography** exhibitions – they are inspiration for my own work.
- Street art and graffiti are modern forms of art in public space.

## Cultural life in my town

- Brno offers theatres, cinemas, clubs, galleries (Moravian Gallery), festivals and a vibrant café culture.
- Cultural events during the year: fireworks festival Ignis Brunensis, Christmas markets, open-air summer cinema.
- In villages culture means balls, fire brigade dances, local festivals (hody) – smaller but more personal.

## Useful vocabulary

- **to attend a cultural event** – navštívit kulturní akci
- **performance / play** – představení / divadelní hra
- **audience** – publikum
- **box office** – pokladna
- **admission / ticket** – vstupné / vstupenka
- **exhibition** – výstava
- **experience** – zážitek
- **to dress up** – společensky se obléknout

## Questions to practise

- What cultural events do you like going to?
- When did you last go to the cinema or theatre? What did you see?
- Do you prefer watching films at home or in the cinema? Why?
- What kind of music do you listen to? Have you ever been to a concert or festival?
- What cultural life does your town offer?
