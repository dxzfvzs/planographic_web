# Global Problems

Our world faces problems that **no country can solve alone** – they cross all borders. In this topic I should name the main global problems, explain them simply, and say **what ordinary people (including me) can do**.

---

## Environmental problems

- **Climate change / global warming** – caused by **greenhouse gases** (CO₂) from burning coal, oil and gas; results: extreme weather, **droughts, floods**, melting glaciers, rising sea levels.
- **Pollution** – of air (traffic, factories), water (plastics in the oceans!) and soil.
- **Waste** – we produce mountains of rubbish; **plastic waste** is the worst because it lasts hundreds of years.
- **Deforestation** – cutting down rainforests (the Amazon); **endangered species** are losing their homes.
- Solutions: **renewable energy** (solar, wind), **recycling**, public transport and electric cars, protecting nature, international agreements.

## Social and economic problems

- **Poverty and hunger** – millions of people live on less than a few dollars a day; lack of **clean drinking water**, healthcare and education, child labour.
- **Wars and terrorism** – armed conflicts destroy countries and lives (Ukraine, the Middle East).
- **Refugees and migration** – people escape from war and poverty; host countries debate how to help and integrate them.
- **Diseases and pandemics** – COVID-19 showed how connected the world is; in poor countries people die of curable diseases.
- **Overpopulation** – 8 billion people and growing → more food, water and energy needed.
- Other issues: racism and discrimination, drugs, fake news and internet dangers.

## Who tries to help?

- International organisations: the **UN (United Nations)**, WHO, UNICEF; the EU,
- **charities and NGOs**: Red Cross, Doctors Without Borders, Greenpeace; Czech ones: Člověk v tísni (People in Need),
- activists and movements (Fridays for Future).

## What can I do?

- **sort and recycle waste** (paper, plastic, glass – as a printing student I know paper can be recycled 5–7 times!),
- save **energy and water**, don't waste **food**,
- walk, cycle or use **public transport**,
- buy less, choose **second-hand** and local products, avoid fast fashion,
- donate to charity, volunteer, stay informed and **vote**,
- small steps by millions of people make a difference.

## Useful vocabulary

- **climate change** – změna klimatu
- **greenhouse gases** – skleníkové plyny
- **pollution** – znečištění
- **drought / flood** – sucho / povodeň
- **renewable energy** – obnovitelná energie
- **poverty** – chudoba
- **refugee** – uprchlík
- **charity** – charita, dobročinná organizace
- **to make a difference** – něco změnit

- What causes climate change and what are its results?
